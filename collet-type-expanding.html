Collet Type Expanding Mandrel is a clamping solution widely used in turning, milling, grinding, and inspection operations. It is especially suited for the gear-cutting sector, where unique requirements, part variation, and smaller lot sizes make finding the right clamping device challenging. Unlike conventional fixtures, these mandrels allow uniform internal clamping so that the outer surface remains free for machining.
